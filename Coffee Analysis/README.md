# Coffee Sales Analysis Dashboard

**Power BI Project | Retail & Consumer Analytics**

---

## Introduction

In the competitive coffee retail industry, understanding sales patterns, customer preferences, and revenue drivers is essential for business growth and operational efficiency. Coffee shops and chains generate large volumes of transactional data daily, but raw data alone provides limited value without proper analysis and visualization.

This project presents a comprehensive **Coffee Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor overall performance, identify top-selling products, understand daily and monthly trends, and evaluate payment behavior.

By leveraging advanced data modeling and visualization techniques in Power BI, this project demonstrates how business intelligence tools can turn simple sales records into strategic decision-support systems. The analysis covers a full year of coffee sales data, providing valuable perspectives on product performance, revenue distribution, and operational patterns that can support inventory planning, marketing strategies, and business optimization.

---

## Tools Used

- **Microsoft Power BI Desktop** — Primary tool for data modeling, DAX development, and interactive dashboard creation
- **Power Query Editor** — Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** — Creation of custom measures and KPIs
- **Microsoft Excel** — Intermediate data preparation and cleaning before importing into Power BI
- **Kaggle Dataset** — Source of the original coffee sales data

---

## Objective

The main objective of this project is to build a professional, interactive analytics solution that provides clear visibility into coffee sales performance. 

Specific goals include:
- Analyzing overall revenue and transaction volume
- Identifying the best-performing coffee products
- Understanding daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends
- Evaluating the impact of payment methods (card vs cash)
- Delivering actionable insights through a clean and user-friendly dashboard

---

## Overview of Work

This project resulted in a professional multi-page Power BI dashboard focused on coffee sales performance. The report includes:

- **KPI Cards** displaying key metrics at a glance (Total Revenue, Total Transactions, Card Transactions, and Average Order Value)
- **Revenue by Coffee Type** — Horizontal bar chart highlighting the contribution of each coffee variant
- **Revenue Trends** — Monthly and daily revenue analysis to identify patterns and seasonality
- **Transactions by Day** — Visualization of transaction volume across different days
- **Average Order Value by Weekday** — Comparison of order values between weekends and weekdays
- **Interactive Filters** — Slicers for year, quarter, payment type, and coffee category for dynamic analysis

The dashboard was designed with a clean, modern aesthetic using professional color schemes and consistent formatting to ensure clarity and ease of use for business stakeholders.

---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

1. **Data Acquisition**  
   Downloaded the Coffee Sales dataset from Kaggle containing transactional records with date, payment type, amount, and coffee product information.

2. **Data Preparation in Excel**  
   Performed initial data cleaning, formatting, and basic transformations in Microsoft Excel to ensure data quality before importing into Power BI.

3. **Data Import and Transformation (Power Query)**  
   Imported the cleaned dataset into Power BI and used Power Query to further clean the data, create calculated columns, and build a proper data model with date and product dimensions.

4. **Data Modeling**  
   Established relationships between tables and created a calendar table to support time-based analysis.

5. **DAX Development**  
   Created custom measures for key metrics including Total Revenue, Total Transactions, Average Order Value, and various comparative calculations.

6. **Dashboard Design and Visualization**  
   Built interactive visuals including KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, and area charts. Applied professional formatting and theming for a polished appearance.

7. **Testing and Optimization**  
   Validated all measures and visuals for accuracy. Optimized the report for performance and responsiveness.

8. **Final Review**  
   Reviewed the complete dashboard for clarity, consistency, and professional presentation.
